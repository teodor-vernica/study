Training

Throught this study, you will be working with a Shaft design as a case study. The goal is to try to understand its environmental footprint, identify redesign opportunities to reduce its footprint, and compare it to a different product from the same product family.

The tabs above contain various information regarding the studied design. In this training task, and the final task, two designs will be presented. Therefore, each data tab will have two additional tabs; one for Design A and one for Design B. Selecting each tab will show the data relevent to its respective design.

3D Model controls: rotate 3D models using the left mouse button, translate the model using the right mouse button, and zoom in and out using the scroll wheel.

Navigate through the tabs above to access varous data about the design.

Each task will have a number of questions in the panel on the left. Please answer all questions to the best of your knowledge before proceeding to the next question.

Once you are done familiarizing yourself with the study interface, type "ready" in the answer box in the left panel and click next to move on to the first task.
